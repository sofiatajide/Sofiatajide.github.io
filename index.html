Career

I’ve always been driven by a passion for technology, data, and problem-solving, which has shaped my diverse and impactful career journey. Beginning with a foundation in Civil Engineering, I cultivated strong analytical and project management skills that have since evolved into expertise in product management, data analysis, and operations research.
My professional experience includes roles as a Product Manager at Yuvoice and Hack for LA, where I refined user stories, designed dashboards, and implemented innovative solutions to streamline processes and enhance user experiences. At LOCOMeX, as a Product Manager Intern, I led cross-functional teams and drove project efficiency through agile practices.

Earlier in my career, I honed my communication, leadership, and organizational skills as a Lecturer, guiding students through complex concepts, managing projects, and conducting research to improve engineering practices. As a Graduate Assistant at NYU, I enhanced my attention to detail, data management, and process optimization by auditing applications and ensuring accuracy in high-volume, fast-paced environments.
My career is guided by a commitment to continuous learning and growth. Today, I’m focused on leveraging my skills in technology and data to deliver impactful solutions and drive meaningful change. My ultimate goal is to collaborate with innovative teams, solve complex challenges, and lead initiatives that create lasting impact.
